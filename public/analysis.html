About the Visualization

This interactive dashboard visualizes air quality data across different regions, focusing on pollution levels, health impacts, and associated economic and energy metrics.

World Map

A choropleth map displays the air quality index for various countries, color-coded by pollution levels (low, moderate, unhealthy, or hazardous). Users can navigate through different years, zoom in, and search for specific countries to analyze trends over time.

Country-Specific Details

  • Pollution: PM2.5 exposure levels and CO‚ÇÇ emissions from transport, industrial processes, and combustion.
  • Health: Key health indicators such as life expectancy and tuberculosis incidence rates.
  • Economy: Metrics like GDP per capita and economic growth trends.
  • Energy: Renewable energy consumption and fossil fuel usage.

This visualization provides a comprehensive overview of air quality and its broader implications, enabling users to explore trends and make data-driven insights.
